First construction client generated: Silva Construções (Coimbra, Portugal, 2026-07-14).

**Color palette that worked:**
- Primary: `#1E3A8A` (deep navy blue) — conveys solidity and trust
- Secondary: `#F59E0B` (amber/golden) — energy, craftsmanship, premium feel
- Accent: `#64748B` (slate) — professional neutrality

**Font personality:** Sans-serif heading + readable body; the palette is already distinctive enough without exotic type choices.

**Tagline formula that worked:** "[Verb de construção]. [Verb de confiança]." — e.g. "Construímos com qualidade. Entregamos com confiança."

**Effective hero headline (Portuguese):** "Transformamos Espaços. Construímos Futuros." — paired with a subheadline citing years of experience and geographic area.

**Services structure (4-item sweet spot for construction):**
1. Remodelação Residencial — kitchens, bathrooms, extensions, finishes
2. Construção Comercial — offices, retail, restaurants, light industrial
3. Reabilitação de Edifícios — structural consolidation, facades, energy efficiency, waterproofing
4. Projetos Chave-na-Mão — architecture + licensing + coordination + optional interior design

**Portfolio mix (6 items):** 3 residential / 2 commercial / 1 heritage rehabilitation. Alternate accent colors between primary (#1E3A8A) and secondary (#F59E0B); use accent (#64748B) sparingly for variety.

**Team (3 members for mid-size firm):**
- Founder/CEO (Engenheiro Civil — FCTUC background adds credibility)
- Architect (FCTUC/reabilitação specialisation)
- Site Manager (Gestor de Obras — 15 years field experience)

**FAQ topics universally relevant to Portuguese construction:**
- Licenciamento camarário process and timescales
- IMI/IFRRU 2020 incentives for rehabilitation
- Warranty periods (Decreto-Lei 84/2008 — 5 years structural)
- How turnkey pricing works vs. direct management
- Timeframes per intervention type

**Contact form fields that converted (PT construction):**
- Nome (text, required)
- Email (email, required)
- Telefone (tel, optional — many prefer WhatsApp contact)
- Tipo de Serviço (select, required — mirror the 4 service names exactly)
- Descrição do Projeto (textarea, required — prompt for area, location, timeline)
- Orçamento Previsto (select, optional) — ranges: <€5k / €5k–€20k / €20k–€100k / >€100k

**Coimbra-specific details:**
- Area code: +351 239 (Coimbra landline prefix)
- Surrounding areas relevant for portfolio: Condeixa-a-Nova, Penela, Figueira da Foz, Lousã
- Heritage body for protected buildings: DRCC (Direção Regional de Cultura do Centro)
- Key certifications to mention: RECS (energy certification), NP EN norms
- Funding programme worth mentioning: IFRRU 2020 (urban rehabilitation incentive)
